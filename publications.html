Research Focus Areas

C. elegans Research

Investigating the neuroprotective effects of plant-derived compounds using C. elegans as a model organism. This research aims to identify and characterize bioactive compounds that may contribute to novel pharmacological applications.

Plant Research

Analyzing the nutritional composition and antioxidant properties of various plant samples, including seaweeds and dates. This research supports the development of functional foods and nutraceuticals from locally available plant resources.

Bioinformatics Research

Utilizing computational and systems biology approaches to accelerate drug discovery, optimize vaccine design, and perform comprehensive pan-cancer analysis. Our work integrates multi-omics data to uncover novel therapeutic targets.
